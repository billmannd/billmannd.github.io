Dan Billmann

I chose the University of Cincinnati because of its experiental learning program, its location relative to the urban center of Cincinnati, and the wide range of academic and athletic programs. Without a doubt this was the best choice I could have made for college.

I am blessed to be a current student in the Lindner Honors-PLUS program, a competitive business program in the Lindner College of Businesss. Through this incredible program I spent six weeks learning about European Business Practices at Credit Suisse, Bloomberg, Audi, and the World Trade Organization while immersing myself in the culture of London, Brussels, Munich, Milan, Riga, and Geneva. I also completed six co-ops/internships over the course of my time in the program, giving me over two years work experience upon graduation.



The Honors PLUS program encourages its students to be leaders on and off campus and I pursued this in several ways. Most notably, I founded the Bearcat Developers, a group that helps students connect with the digital world through programming. I also was a member, Treasurer, and later President of the Men's Volleyball Club, travelling to Dallas, Reno, and Louisville to compete in the national club tournament.
